## 3. Data We Collect

### 3.1 Personal Information
When you register in the app, we collect:
- **Full Name**
- **Email Address**
- **Phone Number**
- **Date of Birth**
- **Gender**
- **Profile Picture** (optional)

### 3.2 Health Data
The app collects the following health data:
- **Heart Rate**: Measured using your phone's camera and flash
- **Medication Records**: Medications you take and their schedules
- **Medical Appointments**: Your bookings with doctors
- **Emergency Contacts**

**Important Note:** Health data is highly sensitive and is stored securely and encrypted. We do not share this data with any third party without your explicit consent.

### 3.3 Location Data
- **Precise Location**: To determine your location on the map
- **Background Location**: To track your location in emergencies and send alerts to emergency contacts

**Why do we need background location?**  
To ensure your safety, the app needs to know your location even when you're not actively using the app, to send your location to emergency contacts when needed.

### 3.4 Photos and Files
- **Camera Photos**: To capture profile picture
- **Gallery Photos**: To select profile picture

### 3.5 Usage Data
We collect information about how you use the app:
- **Pages you visit**
- **Features you use**
- **Usage time**
- **Errors and technical issues**

### 3.6 Device Information
- **Device Type**
- **Operating System and Version**
- **Unique Device Identifier**
- **IP Address**
